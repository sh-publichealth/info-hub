The Recruitment Process

Part 1. First contact

NotePart 1 Notes

R1. Use the REDCap “Recruitment Report”. Choose and contact the next person on the list.

R2/R3. For potential participants responding to the project advert or recruited from clinic, search the REDCap database to see if they exist. If not on the database, create and complete a new Screening Record for this person.

R4. Search the REDCap database for the person in question. You will use the person’s REDCap Screening Form during the recruitment process.

Part 2. Participant Choices

NotePart 2 Notes

R5. You will of course only make contact if the person already meets the age, diabetes duration, and recent clinic visit criteria (see Eligibility for details). They will still need height and weight measurement (for the calculation of Body Mass Index) to confirm final eligibility.

R6. If interested, book a screening visit using the REDCap visit scheduling tool. Point the person to the online Information Sheet.

R7. If possibly interested, point the participant to the online information sheet. And schedule a follow-up call.

R8. If not interested, the person could still be part of our control group. Follow the possible control telephone script.

R9. For each possible outcome, updates to the REDCap Screening Form will be required. Follow the project REDCap guidance on this.
