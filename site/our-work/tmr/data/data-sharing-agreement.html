1. Purpose of this agreement

This agreement sets out the terms under which data from the Total Meal Replacement Study will be shared.

Its purpose is to ensure that data are used responsibly, ethically, and in ways that respect the people and communities represented.

4. Permitted use

The Data Recipient agrees to use the data only for the following purpose(s):

____________________________________________________________________

The data must not be used to identify individuals or to make decisions about individuals.

5. Data protection and security

The Data Recipient agrees to:

  • Store the data securely
  • Limit access to authorised individuals only
  • Not attempt to re-identify individuals
  • Comply with applicable data protection laws
  • Notify the Data Provider of any data breach or misuse

6. Further sharing

The Data Recipient must not share the data with any third party without written permission from the Data Provider.

7. Publication and acknowledgement

Any reports, publications, or outputs using the data must:

  • Acknowledge the Data Provider and the project
  • Avoid presenting results that could lead to identification of individuals or small groups

9. Termination

The Data Provider may withdraw permission to use the data if the terms of this agreement are breached.

Upon termination, the Data Recipient agrees to securely delete or return the data, as instructed.
